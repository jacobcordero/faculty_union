A united and independent faculty union that cares for the rights and welfare of the WMSU FACULTY, with strong collective voice gearing towards equitable access to professional development , opportunities, and healthy working environment.

OBJECTIVES

1. To defend and advance academic freedom and shared governance in WMSU;

2. To foster solidarity, collaboration, linkages, partnership, and sense of community;
